\opening{Dear Editor(s),}

please find attached the manuscript ``Structured climate financing:
valuation of CDO's on inhomogeneous asset pools''.

In this paper I investigate a class of structured funds that has
recently emerged. These structured funds are public-private
partnerships seeking to leverage investment in renewable energy in
emerging markets by attracting institutional investors. 

The main contributions lie in explaining the cash-flow mechanism of
these funds, deriving analytical pricing formulas for valuing
tranches, and showing how to optimally structure the tranches. 

The paper contains original research, has not been published and is
not being considered for publication elsewhere.
%\enlargethispage{5\baselineskip}

I would be grateful if you could consider the paper for publication in
SN Business \& Economics. Thank you for your consideration.
